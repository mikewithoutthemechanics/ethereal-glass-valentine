# Ambient Audio Guide (Cinematic Edition)

## Add a piano track for cinematic ambience

1. **Find a royalty-free piano piece** (try [Free Music Archive](https://freemusicarchive.org/), [Incompetech](https://incompetech.com/), or YouTube Audio Library)
2. **Download as MP3** (~2–3 minutes, loopable)
3. **Rename to** `ambient.mp3`
4. **Copy to** `ethereal-glass-valentine/assets/audio/ambient.mp3`

When the envelope opens, the piano will fade in gently and loop until the browser tab closes.

**Recommended mood:** soft, minimal, impressionist — like Debussy or Ludovico Einaudi. Something that feels like falling in love.
